1. OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Afro Genie is Africa's premier AI-powered lyric translation and cultural 
annotation platform. It provides:

- The largest library of African lyrics in multiple languages
- AI-powered translations that preserve cultural context
- Community-driven discussions and cultural insights
- Artist tools for managing their content
- Comprehensive admin management system

The platform supports various African languages and dialects, translating them
into global languages while maintaining the original meaning, context, and
cultural significance.

================================================================================
2. GETTING STARTED
================================================================================

2.1 ACCESSING THE APPLICATION
-----------------------------
- Website URL: https://afrogenie.web.app
- No account required to browse and search
- Sign in required for: saving favorites, creating topics, requesting translations

2.2 CREATING AN ACCOUNT
-----------------------
1. Click "Sign In" button in the top right corner
2. Choose your preferred sign-in method:
   - Google Account (recommended)
   - Email and password
3. Complete your profile information
4. You're ready to use all features!

2.3 FIRST TIME USER GUIDE
--------------------------
1. Start at the homepage to explore trending artists and genres
2. Use the search bar to find specific songs or artists
3. Click on any song to view lyrics and translations
4. Join the community forum to discuss music and culture
5. Save your favorite songs for quick access later

================================================================================
3. MAIN FEATURES FOR REGULAR USERS
================================================================================

3.1 HOMEPAGE
------------
The homepage is your starting point with:

- SEARCH BAR: Search for artists, songs, languages, or genres
- TRENDING ARTISTS: Browse popular African artists with their images
- EXPLORE BY GENRE: Discover music by genre categories
- TRENDING DISCUSSIONS: See the most popular community topics
- FLOATING GENIE: Animated mascot that can be customized by admins

How to Use:
- Click on any artist card to search for their songs
- Click on any genre to filter by that genre
- Use the search bar for specific queries
- Click "View All" on trending discussions to join the community

3.2 SEARCH FUNCTIONALITY
-------------------------
The search feature is available in the header and homepage.

Search Capabilities:
- Search by Artist Name
- Search by Song Title
- Search by Genre
- Search by Language

How to Use:
1. Type your search query in the search bar
2. Press Enter or click the search icon
3. Results will show matching artists, songs, and genres
4. Click on any result to view details

3.3 VIEWING SONGS AND LYRICS
-----------------------------
When you click on a song, you'll see:

- Song Title and Artist Information
- Original Lyrics (in the original language)
- Translated Lyrics (with cultural context)
- Language Tabs: Switch between Original and Translation views
- Action Bar with:
  * Like/Unlike button (save to favorites)
  * Share button
  * Discuss button (link to community forum)
  * Font size adjustment

How to Use:
1. Navigate to a song page
2. Use tabs to switch between Original and Translation
3. Click the heart icon to add to favorites (requires sign-in)
4. Click Share to share the song with others
5. Click Discuss to start or join a discussion about the song

3.4 REQUESTING TRANSLATIONS
----------------------------
Request AI-powered translations for songs not yet in the database.

How to Use:
1. Click "Request Translation" in the header or homepage
2. Fill in the form:
   - Song Title (required)
   - Artist Name (required)
   - Original Language (select from dropdown)
   - Target Language (select from dropdown)
   - Original Lyrics (paste the lyrics)
   - Additional Context (optional - cultural notes, background info)
3. Click "Generate Translation"
4. The AI will process your request and provide:
   - Translated lyrics
   - Cultural context explanations
   - Meaning preservation notes
5. Review and submit if satisfied

Features:
- AI-powered translation using Gemini AI
- Cultural context preservation
- Multiple language support
- Context-aware translations

3.5 FAVORITES AND HISTORY
--------------------------
Save songs you love and track your viewing history.

Favorites:
- Click the heart icon on any song page
- Access your favorites from your profile
- Remove favorites by clicking the heart again

History:
- Automatically tracks songs you've viewed
- Access from your profile menu
- Helps you find recently viewed content

How to Access:
1. Sign in to your account
2. Click your profile icon in the top right
3. Select "Favorites" or "History" from the menu

3.6 SHARING SONGS
-----------------
Share songs with friends and family.

How to Use:
1. Navigate to any song page
2. Click the "Share" button in the action bar
3. Choose your sharing method:
   - Copy link to clipboard
   - Share via native share dialog (mobile)
   - Share on social media platforms

4. COMMUNITY FORUM FEATURES
================================================================================

The community forum is where users discuss music, share experiences, and
connect with fellow music lovers.

4.1 ACCESSING THE COMMUNITY
----------------------------
- Click "Community" in the navigation menu
- Or visit: /community
- No sign-in required to browse, but required to post

4.2 FORUM CATEGORIES
--------------------
The forum is organized into categories:
- General Discussion
- Translation Requests
- Cultural Insights
- Artist Spotlights
- And more (customizable by admins)

Each category shows:
- Category name and description
- Number of topics
- Latest topic preview

4.3 CREATING A TOPIC
---------------------
Start a new discussion thread.

How to Create:
1. Click "Create Topic" button (top right of community page)
2. Fill in the topic form:
   - Title (required)
   - Content/Description (required)
   - Category (select from dropdown)
   - Link to Song (optional - if discussing a specific song)
   - Link to Artist (optional - if discussing an artist)
   - Upload Image (optional)
3. Click "Create Topic"
4. Your topic will appear in the selected category

Quick Create from Song Page:
- Click "Discuss" button on any song page
- The form will be pre-filled with song and artist information
- Just add your thoughts and submit

4.4 VIEWING TOPICS
------------------
Browse topics by:
- Category (filter by specific category)
- Latest (most recent first)
- Most Liked (most popular)
- Most Discussed (most comments)

How to View:
1. Go to Community page
2. Click on a category to see all topics in that category
3. Or browse all topics using filters
4. Click on any topic card to read the full discussion

4.5 TOPIC DETAILS PAGE
----------------------
When viewing a topic, you can see:

- Full topic content
- Author information and avatar
- Creation date
- Like count
- Comment count
- Share count
- Linked song/artist (if applicable)
- All comments and replies

Actions Available:
- Like the topic (click heart icon)
- Share the topic
- Comment on the topic
- Reply to comments
- Like comments

4.6 COMMENTING
--------------
Engage in discussions by commenting.

How to Comment:
1. Navigate to a topic detail page
2. Scroll to the comment section
3. Type your comment in the text box
4. Click "Post Comment"
5. Your comment will appear immediately

Replying to Comments:
1. Click "Reply" on any comment
2. Type your reply
3. Click "Post Reply"
4. Replies are nested under the original comment

Liking Comments:
- Click the heart icon on any comment
- Shows appreciation for helpful comments

4.7 TOPIC ACTIONS
-----------------
Like Topics:
- Click the heart icon to like a topic
- See total likes count
- Unlike by clicking again

Share Topics:
- Click the share icon
- Share via link or social media

4.8 USER PROFILE IN COMMUNITY
------------------------------
Your community profile shows:

- Profile picture and name
- Post count (topics you've created)
- Comment count (comments you've made)
- Reputation score
- Badges earned
- Quick actions:
  * Request Translation
  * Start Discussion
  * Admin Panel (if you're an admin)

4.9 USER BADGES
---------------
Earn badges for community participation:
- First Post badge
- Active Contributor badge
- Helpful Translator badge
- Community Leader badge
- And more (awarded by admins)

View badges on your profile card in the community section.

5. ARTIST FEATURES
================================================================================

Artists have special tools to manage their content on the platform.

5.1 ARTIST SIGNUP
-----------------
Artists can create a dedicated account.

How to Sign Up:
1. Click "Artist Signup" or visit /artist/signup
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - Artist Name
   - Email Address
   - Password
   - Genre
   - Bio
   - Profile Image (optional)
3. Submit for approval
4. Once approved, access the Artist Dashboard

5.2 ARTIST DASHBOARD
--------------------
Access your dashboard at /artist/dashboard (requires artist account)

Features Available:
- View Your Songs: See all your songs in the database
- Add New Songs: Submit new songs with lyrics
- Manage Lyrics: Edit or update existing lyrics
- View Statistics: See views, likes, and engagement
- Update Profile: Edit your artist information
- Request Translations: Request translations for your songs

How to Use:
1. Sign in with your artist account
2. Navigate to Artist Dashboard
3. Use the sidebar to access different sections
4. Manage your content and track performance

5.3 SUBMITTING SONGS AS AN ARTIST
----------------------------------
Artists can directly submit their songs.

Steps:
1. Go to Artist Dashboard
2. Click "Add New Song"
3. Fill in song details:
   - Song Title
   - Lyrics (original language)
   - Language
   - Genre
   - Release Date
   - Album (optional)
4. Submit for review
5. Once approved, your song appears in the database

5.4 REQUESTING TRANSLATIONS AS AN ARTIST
----------------------------------------
Artists can request translations for their songs.

Steps:
1. Go to Artist Dashboard
2. Select a song
3. Click "Request Translation"
4. Specify target language
5. Add cultural context if needed
6. Submit request
7. Translation will be generated and added to your song

Administrators have full control over the platform. Access requires admin role.

6.1 ACCESSING ADMIN PANEL
-------------------------
1. Sign in with an admin account
2. Click your profile icon

6.2 ADMIN DASHBOARD
-------------------
Overview of platform statistics:

- Total Songs
- Total Genres
- Total Users

-------------------
Manage all artists in the database.

Features:

- Add new artists
- Edit existing artists
- Delete artists
- Search and filter artists

How to Use:
1. Go to Admin Panel > Artists
2. Click "Add Artist" to create new
3. Click edit icon to modify existing

5. Use search bar to find specific artists

6.4 SONGS MANAGER
-----------------
Manage all songs in the database.

Features:
- View all songs
- Add new songs
- Edit song details
- Delete songs
- Link songs to artists
- Link songs to genres
- Search and filter songs

How to Use:
1. Go to Admin Panel > Songs
2. Use filters to find songs
3. Click "Add Song" to create new
4. Edit or delete as needed

6.5 GENRES MANAGER
------------------
Manage music genres.

Features:
- View all genres
- Add new genres
- Edit genre information
- Delete genres
- Upload genre images
- Set genre descriptions

6.6 COMMUNITY MANAGER
--------------------
Moderate the community forum.

Features:
- View all topics
- Edit topics
- Delete topics
- Pin/unpin topics (featured topics)
- Lock/unlock topics (prevent new comments)
- View comments for each topic
- Delete comments
- Manage categories:
  * Create new categories
  * Edit categories
  * Delete categories
  * Set category icons and order

How to Use:
1. Go to Admin Panel > Community
2. Switch between tabs:
   - Topics: Manage all discussion topics
   - Categories: Manage forum categories
   - Comments: View and moderate comments
3. Use filters to find specific content
4. Take moderation actions as needed

Topic Moderation:
- Pin: Makes topic appear at top of category
- Lock: Prevents new comments (useful for resolved discussions)
- Edit: Modify topic title or content
- Delete: Remove topic permanently

6.7 USERS MANAGER
------------------
Manage user accounts and roles.

Features:
- View all users
- Edit user information
- Change user roles (user, moderator, admin)
- View user activity
- Search users by email or name

User Roles:
- User: Regular member
- Moderator: Can moderate community content
- Admin: Full platform access

6.8 UNIFIED MANAGER
-------------------
Import content from external APIs.

Features:
- Import artists from external sources
- Import songs with metadata
- Import lyrics from various sources
- Resolve duplicates
- Batch import operations

Supported Sources:
- Genius API
- MusicBrainz
- Last.fm
- LyricFind
- And more (configurable)

6.9 GENIE MANAGER
-----------------
Customize the floating genie mascot on the homepage.

Features:
- Upload custom genie image
- Set animation type (float, bounce, pulse, spin, none)
- Adjust animation duration (1-10 seconds)
- Set animation delay (0-5 seconds)
- Control background opacity (0-100%)
- Set genie size (small, medium, large)
- Live preview of changes

How to Use:
1. Go to Admin Panel > Genie Settings
2. Upload a new image or adjust settings
3. Use the live preview to see changes
4. Click "Save Settings" to apply

6.10 LYRIC IMPORTER
-------------------
Bulk import lyrics into the system.

Features:
- Import from text files
- Import from CSV
- Batch processing
- Automatic artist/song matching
- Duplicate detection

6.11 LYRICS MANAGER
-------------------
Manage all lyrics and translations.

Features:
- View all lyrics
- Edit lyrics
- Add translations
- Delete lyrics
- Link to songs
- Search lyrics

9. TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

9.1 IMAGES NOT LOADING
----------------------
Problem: Images (genie, artists, etc.) not showing

Solutions:
- Hard refresh browser (Cmd+Shift+R or Ctrl+Shift+R)
- Clear browser cache
- Check internet connection
- Try different browser
- Contact support if issue persists

9.2 CAN'T SIGN IN
-----------------
Problem: Unable to sign in

Solutions:
- Check email and password
- Try "Forgot Password" if available
- Use Google sign-in as alternative
- Clear browser cookies
- Check if account exists

9.3 SEARCH NOT WORKING
----------------------
Problem: Search returns no results

Solutions:
- Check spelling
- Try different search terms
- Use broader terms
- Check if content exists in database
- Try searching by artist name instead

9.4 TRANSLATION NOT GENERATING
------------------------------
Problem: Translation request fails

Solutions:
- Check internet connection
- Verify lyrics are provided
- Try again (AI may be processing)
- Check if languages are supported
- Contact support if persistent

9.5 CAN'T POST TOPIC/COMMENT
----------------------------
Problem: Unable to create topic or comment

Solutions:
- Ensure you're signed in
- Check if topic is locked (admins can lock)
- Verify you have permission
- Try refreshing page
- Clear browser cache

9.6 ADMIN PANEL ACCESS DENIED
------------------------------
Problem: Can't access admin features

Solutions:
- Verify you have admin role
- Sign out and sign back in
- Check with system administrator
- Verify role in Firebase Console

10. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Do I need an account to use Afro Genie?
A: No, you can browse, search, and view lyrics without an account. Sign-in is
   required for saving favorites, creating topics, and requesting translations.

Q: How accurate are the translations?
A: Translations use advanced AI that preserves cultural context. However, they
   are AI-generated and may benefit from human review for perfect accuracy.

Q: Can I contribute translations?
A: Yes! You can request translations and participate in the community forum
   to discuss and improve translations.

Q: How do I become an artist on the platform?
A: Use the Artist Signup page to register. Your account will be reviewed and
   approved by administrators.

Q: Can I upload my own songs?
A: Artists can submit songs through the Artist Dashboard. Regular users can
   request songs to be added through the community forum.

Q: How do I report inappropriate content?
A: Contact administrators through the admin panel or use the report feature
   (if available) on content.

Q: Is the platform free to use?
A: Yes, Afro Genie is free for all users. Some features may require
   registration.

Q: What languages are supported?
A: The platform supports multiple African languages and dialects, with
   translations to global languages. Check the language dropdown for available
   options.

Q: How do I change my profile information?
A: Click your profile icon, then select "Edit Profile" or "Settings" from the
   menu.

Q: Can I delete my account?
A: Contact administrators to request account deletion. This action is
   typically permanent.

Q: How are topics organized?
A: Topics are organized into categories. Admins can create and manage
   categories. You can filter topics by category or view all topics.

Q: What are user badges?
A: Badges are earned through community participation. They appear on your
   profile and recognize contributions like first post, active participation,
   etc.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Use the community forum to ask questions, or contact administrators if you
   have admin access.
